Game Instructions!

The Game Lore

After all wars the world collaped into a massive desert. Inside this massive beast the only thing that could exist is pressure.

Players Movement

Use the arrows left and right to control your character run to the left or right.

Use the up arrow near a wall to climb it.

The down arrow does not do anything... for now!

Press spacebar to jump a moderate humanly possible amount.

Rules! So many, ermmm... few!

You gain points by finishing as close to the end of the song as you can! "Run against da' clock ma' boy" (Hyrule Mad King, 2006).

About the Game Idea

We've always wished to feel music, travel along it within our memories and feelings. This game is a tribute to this desire, a sinestesic feel to music and sound. We try to imagine sound, we figured out how its fisics works but we've never climbed it, jumped over it, exhilirate ourselves by touching it.
